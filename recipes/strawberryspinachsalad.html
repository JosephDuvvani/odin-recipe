Strawberry Spinach Salad

Make A Heathly & Tasty Strawberry Spinach Salad

This strawberry spinach salad is a great way to get kids to eat spinach! Someone brought this salad to a potluck dinner, and I had to have the recipe. Ingredients

Dresssing:

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon minced onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Salad:

  • 1 quart strawberries - cleaned, hulled and sliced
  • 10 ounces fresh spinach - rinsed, dried and torn into bite-size pieces
  • 1/4 cup almonds, blanched and slivered
